ABOUT

I am Laura Scravaglieri. Born an artist. My passion of art has became a way of living. Beside Murals, Fine Arts, Furniture renovations,... I discovered that Art doesn’t have to be displayed just at home. One rainy evening, I was running ideas through my mind for a birthday present for a good friend. I wanted to use my creativity to make something special, colorful and unique like her. Using a few materials around the house, my imagination, a stroke of luck, I was able to create a one of a kind “Clutch”. From that day something incredible happened. There’s not a place she went where people did not ask where they could purchase one. So I went back to work to create a line of these original hand painted purses. To my surprise, soon I was receiving pictures of the clutches traveling all over town. I couldn’t be more happy to see art and fashion merge together, becoming the perfect couple. Every time I see people wearing my clutches, I feel like they are modeling a piece of art. That is why I decided to call this collection a “WALKING CANVAS”. You can rest assured that each piece is one of a kind, made with love and care from my hands to yours.
