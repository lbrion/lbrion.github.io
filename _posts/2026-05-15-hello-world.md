I've spent a lot of time building products. Most of that time, AI was something I used at the edges — a tool I integrated, a vendor I evaluated, a capability I shipped for users. I understood it well enough to make decisions about it, but I didn't understand it deeply.

That's the gap I want to close.

This blog is my public log of that process. I'm starting from a CS background (Stanford, then years in product), so I'm not starting from zero — but there's a lot of ground between "can talk intelligently about transformers" and "can actually build things with them well." I want to get to the second place.

## What I'll write about

Mostly whatever I'm working on: experiments with AI APIs, tools I'm learning, concepts I'm trying to internalize, things I built that didn't work, and occasionally things that did. No particular posting schedule — this updates when I have something worth writing down.

If any of it's useful to someone else along the way, great.
